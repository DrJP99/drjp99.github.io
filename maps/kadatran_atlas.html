Kadatran Atlas


The Continent of Kadatran as it was in the year 3,539 of the Bifuris.

For thousands of years, the Kingdom of Vrotonia has dominated much of Kadatran, and though originally a nomad people from a far away land, their power has remained mostly uncontested. But now, a new power rises from the Desert, the Braxian Empire battles to conquer their enemy to the South. With the help of Dark Wizards, Warlocks and the Traitorous King of Durenha, this goal may yet be achieved. It is up to the King of Vrotonia to find an Ancient Weapon used by his forefathers to prove himself the Rightful King and defeat this Foreign Invasion.
