Notes on the Flag and Key with translations

    Colors:
  • Red: Revolution
  • Yellow: Socialism
  • Purple: Roman people
    Symbols:
  • Hammer: Industrial workers
  • Sickle: Agricultural workers
  • Laurel Wreathe: Glory of Rome
C.P.Q.R: Consilium Populusque Romanus
(Counsil and Roman People)

*C.F.S.R: Consiliaria Foederatiua Socialistica Respublica
(Counsil Ferderative Socialist Republic)

*C.S.R: Consiliaria Socialistica Respublica
(Counsil Socialist Republic)

*A.C.S.R: Autonoma Consiliaria Socialistica Respublica
(Autonomous Counsil Socialist Repoublic)
